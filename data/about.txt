<h1>What is open source software?</h1>
The distribution terms of open-source software must comply with the following criteria:<br> 
1. Free Redistribution<br>
2. Source Code<br>
3. Derived Works<br>
4. Integrity of The Author's Source Code<br>
5. No Discrimination Against Persons or Groups<br>
6. No Discrimination Against Fields of Endeavor<br>
7. Distribution of License<br>
8. License Must Not Be Specific to a Product<br>
9. License Must Not Restrict Other Software<br>
10. License Must Be Technology-Neutral
